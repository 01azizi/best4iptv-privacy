Privacy Policy for Best4IPTV

Last Updated: January 9, 2025

⚠️ Important Notice

Best4IPTV is an IPTV player application only. We do not provide, host, or distribute any IPTV content, subscriptions, or streaming services. Users are solely responsible for the content they access through their own IPTV service providers.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • IPTV Server Credentials: Server URLs, usernames, and passwords (encrypted and stored locally on your device)
  • User Preferences: Language settings, theme preferences, playback settings
  • Favorites and History: Channels you mark as favorites and viewing history (stored locally)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, app version
  • Usage Statistics: Watch time, content types viewed (stored locally for analytics)
  • Location Data: Approximate location for weather feature (optional, can be disabled)

1.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • ❌ Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • ❌ Payment information (the app is completely free)
  • ❌ Browsing history outside the app
  • ❌ Contact lists or other personal data

2. How We Use Your Information

Local Storage Only: All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit, share, or upload your information to any external servers.

Purposes:

  • Functionality: To provide IPTV playback and recording features
  • Personalization: To remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics: To improve app performance (all analytics are local)
  • Weather Feature: To display local weather information (optional)

3. Data Security

  • Encryption: Server credentials are encrypted using Android's Security Crypto library
  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: No cloud synchronization or external backups
  • Data Deletion: Data is deleted when you uninstall the app

4. Permissions Explained

Required Permissions:

  • INTERNET: Required to stream IPTV content from your service provider
  • WAKE_LOCK: Prevents device from sleeping during playback

Optional Permissions:

  • LOCATION: Used only for weather feature (can be denied)
  • STORAGE: Used for recording feature (required only if you use recording)
  • NOTIFICATIONS: Used for recording notifications and program reminders

5. Your Rights

  • Access: View all your data in app settings
  • Delete: Clear all data from app settings or uninstall the app
  • Export: Export server configurations and favorites
  • Disable: Turn off optional features (location, notifications)

6. Children's Privacy

Best4IPTV is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. The app includes parental controls to restrict content access.

7. Content Responsibility

  • Users are solely responsible for the content they access
  • Users must ensure they have legal rights to access their IPTV service
  • We do not endorse, support, or encourage piracy or copyright infringement
  • We do not provide IPTV subscriptions or content

8. Third-Party Services

  • Google Cast: For Chromecast functionality (optional)
  • ExoPlayer: For media playback (no data collection)

We DO NOT use: Analytics services, Advertising networks, Social media integrations

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date and displaying a notice in the app.
