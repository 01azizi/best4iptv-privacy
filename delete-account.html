🗑️ Delete Your Account

⚠️ Warning: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

What Will Be Deleted:

  • Your profile information (name, email)
  • All project participation history
  • Wallet balance and transaction records
  • Usage statistics and test records
  • All notifications and settings

💡 Important: Please withdraw any remaining balance from your wallet before deleting your account. Once deleted, you will not be able to access your funds.

How to Delete Your Account:

  1. Open the TesterHive mobile app
  2. Go to Profile tab (bottom navigation)
  3. Tap on Settings ⚙️
  4. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  5. Read the warning message carefully
  6. Enter your password to confirm
  7. Tap "Confirm Deletion"

Alternative Method - Email Request:

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion via email:

Email: 12testerhive@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Include:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your full name
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

We will process your request within 48 hours and send you a confirmation email.

Data Retention After Deletion:

After account deletion:

  • Immediate: Your account will be deactivated and you cannot log in
  • Within 30 days: All personal data will be permanently deleted
  • Legal compliance: Some transaction records may be retained for 7 years as required by law

Need Help?

If you have questions or need assistance, please contact us at 12testerhive@gmail.com before deleting your account.
